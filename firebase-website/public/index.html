About US

This preliminary assessment uses the pre-trained ZC-COVID19 deep learning model developed at Zewail City IRES Lab. You can upload images of X-Ray scans and state the symptoms then the test will give preliminary assessment, it has been trained to recognize COVID-19 from X-Ray scans also the results depends on the common symptoms stated in World Health Organization. To be NOTED that we do not claim diagnostic performance of our test without a clinical study.
